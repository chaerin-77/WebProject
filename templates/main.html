Vegan

Veganism is a philosophy and way of living which seeks to exclude - as far as is possible and practicable - all forms of exploitation of, and cruelty to, animals for food, clothing, or any other purpose.
and by extension, promotes the development and use of animal-free alternatives for the benefit of animals, humans and the environment.

History

Although the vegan diet was defined early on in The Vegan Society's beginnings in 1944, t was as late as 1949 before Leslie J Cross pointed out that the society lacked a definition of veganism. He suggested "he principle of the emancipation of animals from exploitation by man." This is later clarified as "to seek an end to the use of animals by man for food, commodities, work, hunting, vivisection, and by all other uses involving exploitation of animal life by man."
